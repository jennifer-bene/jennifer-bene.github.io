Introduction

Jewels by Jenny

Jewels by Jenny is a locally owned Jewelry Shop in San Francisco, CA. We specialize in making jewelry that is eco-friendly, ethical, and beautiful. All jewelry is handmade, and customers have the option of coming to the store to make jewelry as well.

Target audience

People use our site to see what we are currently selling at our shop and get information about events that will occur. We also receive visitors to our site that have seen our Instagram and want to learn more about us.

Our customers' goals are to get jewelry they know is well made, well-sourced, and beautiful. We have customers buying for themselves, buying for others, and gathering to make jewelry as a group.
